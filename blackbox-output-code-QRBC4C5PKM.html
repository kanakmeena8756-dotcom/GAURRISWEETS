About Us

Established in the heart of Jaipur, Gauri Sweet has been serving traditional Indian sweets for over two decades. Our recipes are passed down through generations, using only the finest ingredients like ghee, sugar, and nuts. Located in Sector 3, Jagatpura, we're committed to quality and authenticity.

Visit us for festivals, celebrations, or just a sweet treat!
